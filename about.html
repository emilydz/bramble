Bramble is a multidisiplinary musician and artist. He creates music, murals, and much more.

Kevin White 'Bramble' makes music that is layered and atmospheric. Using a range of acoustic and digital instruments, White loops sounds through, on top of, and around, much like the brambles of the forest.
